🌱 Why Fertilizer Matters

Fertilizers provide essential nutrients that plants need to grow strong and healthy.

🧪 Types of Fertilizer

  • 🌿 Organic Fertilizer
  • ⚗️ Chemical Fertilizer
  • 🍌 Compost
  • 🐄 Manure

📅 Fertilizing Schedule

  • 🌿 Leafy Plants — Every 2 weeks
  • 🍅 Vegetables — Every 10 days
  • 🌸 Flowers — Weekly

⚠️ Tips

Do not over-fertilize. Water after applying. Prefer organic.

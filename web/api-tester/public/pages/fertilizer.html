💩 Fertilizer Guide

🌱 Why Fertilizer Matters

Fertilizers provide essential nutrients that plants need to grow strong and healthy. They improve soil fertility and help plants produce better leaves, flowers, and fruits.

🧪 Types of Fertilizer

  • 🌿 Organic Fertilizer Natural nutrients
  • ⚗️ Chemical Fertilizer Fast plant growth
  • 🍌 Compost Eco friendly
  • 🐄 Manure Improves soil health

📅 Fertilizing Schedule

  • 🌿 Leafy Plants Every 2 weeks
  • 🍅 Vegetable Plants Every 10 days
  • 🌸 Flower Plants Once a week

⚠️ Fertilizing Tips

• Do not over-fertilize plants. • Water plants after applying fertilizer. • Use organic fertilizers when possible. • Always follow recommended dosage.
